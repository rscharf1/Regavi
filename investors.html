At Regavi, we know that a great idea is worthless without a great team and that poorly-executed strategy is as bad, if not worse, than having no strategy at all. We hope this information helps you believe in our vision and our team as much as we do.
Product:
Augmented contact book and digital business card focused around automatically updating information and seamlessly mirroring exchanging business cards within the convenience of a phone.

Absolute control over the contact information any person has. Segment the information shared based on the nature of a relationship. Address for friends, LinkedIn for colleagues, and nothing for the unknown.

Simplified organization management for the collection of information, addition and subtraction of new members, and mitigation of paperwork.

Diverse, cross-functional team with wide-array of skills and in-depth industry and customer knowledge.

Experienced leaders, managers, and innovators with strong history of success.
Vision:
To be a connection rather than a communication platform, the “missing link of the internet.” By not being a content platform, we put people rather than digital media back at the center of relationships helping curate interaction rather than consumption.

To judge on connection rather than engagement metrics. Engagement is one-sided. A connection is mutual. Regavi is a PRM -- Personal Relationship Manager -- rather than a contact manager or a CRM (customer relationship manager). Regavi offers control over the information shared between two people and encourages injective (one to one) rather than surjective (one to many) relationships. When communication between two parties takes place, it needs to be individualized and current rather than mass and out-of-touch.

To help people connect better. From people to communities to businesses, that with which people are connected define their lifestyle and who they are. We aim to eliminate friction from exchanging information, staying in touch, and discovering new opportunities.
